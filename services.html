Our Services

Professional-grade consulting delivered through structured 8-week engagements, mentored by industry leaders.

Data-Driven Insights

Market research, competitive intelligence, industry analysis and data-backed strategic recommendations tailored to your sector.

Financial Modelling

Revenue projections, unit economics, scenario analysis and investor-ready financial models to support your business decisions.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Stakeholder Mapping

8-Week Project Timeline

Week 1

Kick-off & Training

Team onboarding, project scoping, research methodology briefing and deliverable planning with the Engagement Manager.

Week 2

Client Meeting

Initial client presentation to align on objectives, key questions, deliverables and timeline expectations.

Weeks 3–4

Deep Research

Primary and secondary research, data collection, market analysis and stakeholder interviews.

Week 5

Mid-Review

Progress review with mentor and client. Refine hypotheses, adjust research direction and validate initial findings.

Weeks 6–7

Synthesis

Analyse findings, develop strategic recommendations, build financial models and prepare final deliverables.

Week 8

Final Presentation

Present findings and recommendations to client stakeholders. Deliver final report and supporting materials.
