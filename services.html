Our Services

Professional-grade consulting delivered through structured 8-week engagements, mentored by industry leaders.

What We Deliver

Market Research & Competitive Intelligence

TAM/SAM/SOM sizing, competitor benchmarking, industry landscaping and data-backed strategic recommendations tailored to your sector.

Financial Modelling

Revenue projections, unit economics, scenario analysis and investor-ready financial models to support fundraising and business decisions.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Market entry planning, customer segmentation, pricing strategy and launch roadmaps for seed to Series A ventures.

Stakeholder & Impact Mapping

Identify key stakeholders, map influence networks and develop engagement strategies — particularly useful for social enterprises and grant applications.
