About CCN London

London's top student-led consulting organisation. Registered in England & Wales.

Most startups can't afford consultants.
We fix that.

CCN London provides professional-grade strategic consulting to early-stage ventures, student-run businesses, and social enterprises — completely pro-bono. Our consultants come from LSE, Imperial, UCL and King's College London. Our mentors come from McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Deloitte, EY, OC&C and Strategy&. We bring the rigour of top-tier consulting to the ventures that need it most but can least afford it.

What Drives Us

Real Output, Not Reports

We don't deliver slide decks that collect dust. Every engagement produces actionable strategy: financial models, GTM playbooks, investor-ready research.

Built by Builders

Our consultants aren't just studying business — they're founding startups, leading societies, and shipping products. We understand what early-stage founders need.

Mentored at the Highest Level

20+ mentors from MBB and Big Four firms review our work and coach our teams. The bar is set by industry professionals — not by us.

Global Network, Local Expertise

Part of Cambridge Consulting Network — with offices across the UK, Hong Kong and Thailand. Our London branch brings deep knowledge of the UK startup ecosystem.
