About CCN London

Data-driven strategic consulting, delivered by London's brightest student talent since 2013.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic excellence and real-world impact by delivering professional-grade strategic consulting to organisations that need it most — at zero cost. We develop the next generation of consultants while creating measurable value for our clients.

Excellence

Every deliverable meets the standard of a top-tier consultancy. Our mentorship from MBB and Big Four professionals ensures quality.

Integrity

We treat every client relationship with the same rigour and confidentiality expected of a professional engagement.

Impact

From startups to social enterprises, our recommendations drive real, actionable outcomes.

Development

We invest in our consultants through structured training, mentorship and hands-on project experience.

Our History

2013

Founded in Cambridge

Cambridge Consulting Network was established as a student-run consultancy at the University of Cambridge.

2015

London Office Launched

Expanded to London, recruiting from LSE, Imperial, UCL and King's College London.

2017

Asia-Pacific Expansion

Offices opened in Hong Kong, Thailand and Taiwan, making CCN a truly global network.

2019

MBB Mentorship Programme

Formalised mentorship partnerships with professionals from McKinsey, Bain, BCG, Deloitte, EY, OC&C and Strategy&.

2022

LSE Generate Partnership

Partnered with LSE Generate to provide consulting to high-growth startups from the LSE entrepreneurship ecosystem.

2024

50+ Projects Milestone

Surpassed 50 completed projects across MNCs, startups and charities. Now the 2nd largest student consultancy globally.
