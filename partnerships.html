Our Partnerships

Strategic partnerships with leading universities and mentorship from top-tier consulting firms.

LSE Generate

CCN London holds a partnership with LSE Generate, the LSE's entrepreneurship programme. This provides a pipeline of high-growth startups seeking strategic consulting. Through this partnership, CCN London delivers tailored market research, financial modelling and go-to-market strategies to ventures emerging from one of the UK's leading entrepreneurial ecosystems.

Imperial White City

CCN London is partnered with Imperial College's White City Innovation District, providing consulting to ventures from Imperial's entrepreneurial ecosystem. The White City campus houses hundreds of startups, researchers and innovators — and CCN London provides the strategic consulting expertise to help them scale.
